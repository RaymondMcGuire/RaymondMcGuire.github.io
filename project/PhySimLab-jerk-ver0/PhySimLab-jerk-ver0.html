Jerk

the rate of acceleration motion

the relationship of time, position and velocity

\begin{align} j & = \frac{a_1-a_0}{t_1-t_0} = \frac{a_1-a_0}{\Delta t} \end{align}

Euler method

\begin{align} a_{n+1} & = a_n + j\Delta t \\ r_{n+1} & = r_n + a_n\Delta t \end{align}

Jerk for 3D

\begin{align} (x_{n+1},y_{n+1},z_{n+1}) & = (x_n + v_xn\Delta t,y_n + v_yn\Delta t,z_n + v_zn\Delta t) \\ (v_{xn+1},v_{yn+1},v_{zn+1}) & = (v_xn + a_xn\Delta t,v_yn + a_yn\Delta t,v_zn + a_zn\Delta t) \\ (a_{xn+1},a_{yn+1},a_{zn+1}) & = (a_xn + j_x\Delta t,a_yn + j_y\Delta t,a_zn + j_z\Delta t) \end{align}
