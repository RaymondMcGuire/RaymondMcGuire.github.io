definiation

Gravity for 3D

\begin{align} F_g & = (0,0,-mg) \\ a(t) & = \frac{F(t)}{m} = (0,0,-g) \end{align}

Newtonian Equation of motion

\begin{align} m(t)a(t) & = F(t) \\ m(t)a_x(t) & = f_x(t) \\ m(t)a_y(t) & = f_y(t) \\ m(t)a_z(t) & = f_z(t) \end{align}

Mechanical energy

left equation

\begin{align} \int_{x_1}^{x_2} m\frac{{\rm d}v(t)}{{\rm d}t} {\rm d}x = & \int_{v_1}^{v_2} m\frac{{\rm d}x(t)}{{\rm d}t} {\rm d}v \\ = & \int_{v_1}^{v_2} mv {\rm d}v \\ = & \left[ \frac{1}{2}mv^2 \right]_{v_1}^{v_2} \\ = & \frac{1}{2}mv_2^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_1^2 \end{align}

right equation

\begin{align} F(t) \equiv &-\frac{{\rm d}U(x(t))}{{\rm d}x} \\ \int_{x_1}^{x_2}F(t){\rm d}x = &-\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{{\rm d}U(x)}{{\rm d}x}{\rm d}x \\ = &-\int_{U_1}^{U_2}{\rm d}U \\ = &-\left[U \right]_{U_1}^{U_2} \\ = &U_1 - U_2 \end{align}

combine left equation and right equation

\[\frac{1}{2}mv_1^2 + U_1 = \frac{1}{2}mv_2^2 + U_2 \]

Kinetic energy

\[K(t) \equiv \frac{1}{2}mv(t)^2 \]

Potential energy

\[U(t) \equiv -\int F(t){\rm d}x \]

Mechanical energy

\[E(t) \equiv U(t)+K(t) \]

Euler Method for 3D FreeFall Simulation

\begin{align} K(t) = & \frac{1}{2}mv(t)^2 \\ = & \frac{1}{2}m(v_x(t)^2 + v_y(t)^2 + v_z(t)^2 ) \end{align}

\begin{align} U(t) = & -\int_0^r F\cdot {\rm d}r \\ = & -\int_0^z (-mg) {\rm d}z \\ = & mgz(t) \end{align}
