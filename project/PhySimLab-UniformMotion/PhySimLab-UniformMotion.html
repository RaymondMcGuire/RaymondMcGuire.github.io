Uniform Motion

Uniform motion is defined as the motion of an object in which the object travels in a straight line and its velocity remains constant along that line as it covers equal distances in equal intervals of time, irrespective of the length of the time.

the relationship of time, position and velocity

\begin{align} v & = \frac{x_1-x_0}{t_1-t_0} = \frac{x_1-x_0}{\Delta t} \\ \Delta & t = t1 - t0 \end{align}

recurrence relation

\[x_1 = x_0 + v\Delta t \]

Euler method

\[x_n+1 = x_n + v\Delta t \]

Uniform Motion for 3D

\begin{align} r_n+1 & = r_n + v\Delta t \\ (x_n+1,y_n+1,z_n+1) & = (x_n + v_x\Delta t,y_n + v_y\Delta t,z_n + v_z\Delta t) \end{align}
